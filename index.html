Installing on macOS

macOS shows a security warning the first time you open ReelBot — this is normal for apps not signed with a paid Apple Developer ID.

⚠️ If macOS says "ReelBot is damaged" or "cannot be opened"

Fastest fix — open Terminal and run:

xattr -cr /Applications/ReelBot.app

Then double-click ReelBot from Applications. The warning will not appear again.

Alternative — right-click the app and choose Open:

  1. Drag ReelBot.app from the DMG into /Applications.
  2. Right-click (or Control-click) ReelBot.app and choose Open.
  3. Click Open again in the dialog. macOS remembers your choice.

Why? ReelBot is open-source and free, so it's not signed with a $99/year Apple Developer ID. The app is safe — you can inspect the source on GitHub.
