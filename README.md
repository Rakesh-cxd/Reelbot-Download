## What is ReelBot?

ReelBot chains your own **Claude/OpenAI** → **ElevenLabs** → **Grok** accounts to produce TikTok/Reels/Shorts-ready videos — all running locally on your machine.

- **Free, BYO-keys, no server.** Nothing is uploaded to us.
- Pick a genre or paste titles → ReelBot writes the script, voices it, generates clips, and stitches them together.
- **~$0.06 per video** (Claude + ElevenLabs usage). Grok is included with your X subscription.

## Install

### macOS

1. Download `ReelBot.dmg`
2. Double-click → drag **ReelBot** into **Applications**
3. First launch: right-click the app → **Open** → click **Open** on the warning dialog
   - Or run: `xattr -d com.apple.quarantine /Applications/ReelBot.app`

### Windows

1. Download `ReelBot.exe`
2. Run the installer
3. Open ReelBot from Start menu or desktop shortcut

---

## First-run setup

1. **API Keys** — paste your keys (stored locally, never leave your machine):
   - [Anthropic (Claude)](https://console.anthropic.com/settings/keys) — recommended
   - [OpenAI](https://platform.openai.com/api-keys) — alternative
   - [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/app/settings/api-keys) — required for voiceover

2. **Connect Grok** — log in to [grok.com/imagine](https://grok.com/imagine) in Chrome, then click **Connect Session** in ReelBot.

3. **Generate** — pick Genre → Video, paste titles, or use a custom template. Click **Start**.

Detailed usage, troubleshooting, and cost breakdown are included in the app after installation.
